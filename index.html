GoGreen

The app's variety of features can help anyone experience the nature around them, and give back to the planet too.

Park Locator

GoGreen provides both a map and a distance-sorted list of nearby parks, anywhere. Open the app in a park, and redeem Green Points!

Activity Tracking

The app, once opened, can tell if you are biking or walking instead of driving - earn Green Points for using human power instead of fossil fuels.

Trail Resources

Through innovative use of the Mashape's TrailAPI, hiking destinations are just a click away.
